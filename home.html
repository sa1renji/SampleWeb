Welcome to Kelly Wood Builder

Kelly Wood Builder is a General Contractor in Amarillo, Texas and now with offices in McKinney, Texas. We operate throughout the Midwest region. Started in 1977, we have come to specialize in healthcare and retail constructions, but remain very active in all types of projects. Our alliances with multiple architectural firms have paved the way to success in Design/Build, Construction Management, and Fast Track construction.
